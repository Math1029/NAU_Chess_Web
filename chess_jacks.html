NAU CHESS JACKS

About

Chess Jacks is a club dedicated to providing chess at Northern Arizona University Flagstaff Campus. It is open to everyone from all walks of life. There are no hidden fees or membership requirements to join. Look for upcoming events at True Blue Connects.

https://nau.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/chessjacks

Meeting

Wednesdays at 5:30pm in Room 3101 of the Health and Learning Center (Building 25).

824 South San Francisco Street, Flagstaff, Arizona 86011

Partners

We partner with Northern Arizona Chess Center for tournaments and events. The events require a United States Chess Federation membership.

https://www.nazchesscenter.org/
