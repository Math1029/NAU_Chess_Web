CHESS JACKS

About

Chess Jacks is a club dedicated to providing chess at Northern Arizona University in Flagstaff, Arizona. It is open to everyone from all walks of life. There are no hidden fees or memberships required to join. Look for upcoming events in True Blue Connects.

https://nau.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/chessjacks

We also have a discord server to chat about topics related to chess.

https://discord.gg/DuthhpQH

Meetings

Chess meetings are in room 3101 (3rd floor) of the Health and Learning Center (Building 25). They start Wednesdays at 5:30pm.

824 South San Francisco Street, Flagstaff, Arizona 86011

Partners

We partner with Northern Arizona Chess Center for tournaments and other events. They provice year round tournaments in Flagstaff, Arizona. However, these tournaments require a United States Chess Federation membership to participate.

https://www.nazchesscenter.org/

Registration is highly recommended to participate in rated chess tournaments. The link below shows how to join and how much it will cost.

https://new.uschess.org/join-us-chess
