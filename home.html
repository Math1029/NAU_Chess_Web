CHESS CLUB @ NAU

Description

Chess Club @ NAU is dedicated to providing chess at Northern Arizona University. It is open to everyone from all walks of life. There are no hidden fees or required memberships to join.

Club Meetings

Chess meetings are located at the Du Bois Center in Aspen B room. They start Mondays at 5:00pm.

306 E Pine Knoll Dr, Flagstaff, AZ 86011

Chess board and pieces are already provided. There is also free pizza.
