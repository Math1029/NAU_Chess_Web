CHESS CLUB at NAU

About

Chess Club at NAU is dedicated to providing chess at Northern Arizona University . It is open for everyone from all walks of life. There are no hidden fees or required memberships to join.

Look for upcoming events on True Blue Connects

We also have Discord

Meetings

Chess meetings are in room 3101 (3rd floor) of the Health and Learning Center (Building 25). Meetings start Wednesdays at 5:30pm.

824 South San Francisco Street, Flagstaff, Arizona 86011

Partners

We partner with Northern Arizona Chess Center for tournaments and other events. The center provides year round tournaments in Flagstaff, Arizona.

A United States Chess Federation membership is required to participate in these rated events.
