Chess Club @ NAU

Chess Club @ NAU is dedicated to providing chess here at Northern Arizona University. It is open to everyone from all walks of life and skill levels. There are no hidden fees or required memberships to join.

At our meetings, you'll have the chance to participate in casual matches, learn new strategies, and connect with others who share your interest in chess. Join us and be part of a community that celebrates the joy of chess in a friendly, relaxed atmosphere.

Location

Our first meeting will occur on September 8th, 2025 at 5:30pm MST in the Frank College of Business room 115.

Chess meetings are located at the Du Bois Center in Aspen B room. They start Mondays at 5:00pm MST.
